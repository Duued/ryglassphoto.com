Reflection
This series of photos was taken in the winter of 2024. The goal was to capture the different stages and the effects of winter weather on plants. I wanted to capture the various looks of flowers and plants during the winter time. This series focuses on using color pop to isolate the subject from the background. My goal was to isolate the background using the aperture, and then take it a step further using color pop to isolate the subject using the editing technique.
