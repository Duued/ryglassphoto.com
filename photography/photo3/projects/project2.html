Reflection
This series focused on combining techniques with reflections, deep depth-of-field, and frozen motion photographs. My original plan was to be able to incorporate more reflections in the photographs, however, it was difficult to get good reflections. There are some minor reflections, but not as many as I was originally hoping for. Although I didn't get the reflections I wanted, I'm still really happy with how the series came out. I shot in RAW to allow me to adjust the colors to my liking. I made no other edits to any of the photographs with the exception of RAW edits. Jake Guzman is a landscape photographer based in Washington. Jake travels to various parks and locations to capture breathtaking photographs.
