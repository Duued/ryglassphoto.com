Reflection
This series of photographs show the beauty of the small world that is often overlooked. The macro lens allowed for me to get extremely close to the subject, and capture the intricate details of the subject. All of the photographs were edited to enhance the colors and details of the photograph. The editing process was done in Affinity Photo.
