Reflection

There were originally 5 photos, 2 of which are broken. I cannot find original copies at this time

For this exercise, I was at Hersheypark, and I took photos of the various lights. These lights were on rides, buildings, and reflected on the water. This allowed me to capture the various colors, patterns, and angles that the lights consisted of. I experimented with my exposure settings to see the different results. I also experimented with the composition of the photos to see how the lights interacted with the environment.
